Introducing Edward de Bono

Edward de Bono was a renowned psychologist and author who pioneered the concept of "lateral thinking," a creative problem-solving technique that encourages unconventional approaches. His methods emphasize breaking free from traditional thought patterns and exploring new perspectives.
